# Elara Incubator Platform - Revenue Model

## Executive Summary

The Elara Incubator Platform generates revenue through a **transparent 10% Citadel Fund allocation** from all business revenue, plus optional premium features and services.

---

## Primary Revenue Stream: Citadel Fund (10%)

### How It Works

```
Business Revenue: $1,000
├── Business Owner (90%): $900
└── Citadel Fund (10%): $100
```

### Revenue Calculation

```
Monthly Revenue = Sum of all business transactions × 10%

Example:
- Business A: $5,000 revenue → $500 to Citadel Fund
- Business B: $3,000 revenue → $300 to Citadel Fund
- Business C: $2,000 revenue → $200 to Citadel Fund
- Business D: $1,500 revenue → $150 to Citadel Fund

Total Citadel Fund: $1,150/month
```

### Variable Costs (Per Transaction)

| Item | Cost |
|------|------|
| Stripe Fee | 2.9% + $0.30 |
| Payment Processing | 0.5% |
| Support (estimated) | 1% |
| Infrastructure (scaling) | 0.5% |
| **Total Variable** | **~4.9% + $0.30** |

### Gross Margin

```
Revenue: $100,000
Stripe Fees (2.9% + $0.30): -$2,900
Variable Costs (2%): -$2,000
Fixed Costs: -$3,500
Gross Profit: $91,600
Gross Margin: 91.6%
```

## Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)

### Calculation

```
Average Revenue per Business: $4,000/month
Citadel Fund (10%): $400/month
Premium Adoption (15%): $15/month
Marketplace Commission (5%): $20/month

Total Revenue per Business: $435/month

Average Customer Lifetime: 24 months
CLV = $435 × 24 = $10,440
```

### Payback Period

```
Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC): $100
CLV: $10,440
Payback Period: 100 / 435 = 2.3 months
```

## Competitive Advantage

### Why This Model Works

1. **Aligned Incentives**
   - We succeed when businesses succeed
   - 10% is fair and transparent
   - Community benefits directly

2. **Low Friction**
   - No upfront fees
   - No monthly subscriptions
   - Pay only when earning

3. **Scalable**
   - Revenue grows with platform
   - Minimal marginal cost
   - High gross margins

4. **Community Building**
