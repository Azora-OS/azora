# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** January 10, 2025

## Introduction

Azora ES (Pty) Ltd ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data.

## Data We Collect

### Account Information
- Name, email address
- Password (encrypted)
- Profile information

### Usage Information
- Login history
- Course progress
- Platform interactions

### Financial Information
- Payment details (via Stripe)
- Transaction history
- Wallet balances

### Technical Information
- IP address
- Browser type
- Device information

## How We Use Data

### Essential Services
- Account management
- Course delivery
- Payment processing
- Security

### Improvements
- Platform optimization
- Feature development
- Bug fixes

### Communications
- Service updates
- Educational content
- Marketing (with consent)

## Data Sharing

We share data only with:
- Service providers (AWS, Stripe, OpenAI)
- Legal requirements
- With your explicit consent

We never sell your data.

## Your Rights

### Access
Request a copy of your data

### Correction
Update inaccurate information

### Deletion
Request account deletion

### Portability
Export your data

### Objection
Opt-out of processing

## Data Security

- Encryption at rest and in transit
- Regular security audits
- Access controls
- Incident response plan

## Data Retention

- Account data: Lifetime + 30 days
- Usage logs: 90 days
- Financial records: 7 years
- Backups: 30 days

## Cookies

We use essential cookies for:
- Authentication
- Security
- Preferences

Analytics cookies require consent.

## Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## Changes

We may update this policy. Users will be notified of significant changes.
