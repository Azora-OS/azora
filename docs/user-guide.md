## User Registration & Authentication

### Creating an Account

1. Click **"Sign Up"** on the homepage
2. Choose your registration method:
   - **Email & Password**: Traditional registration
   - **Google**: Quick sign-in with Google
   - **GitHub**: Sign-in for developers
   - **Apple**: Sign-in on Apple devices

3. Fill in your details:
   - Email address
   - Password (minimum 8 characters)
   - First and last name
   - Accept terms of service

4. **Verify your email** within 24 hours

### Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)

For enhanced security, enable MFA:

1. Go to **Settings > Security**
2. Click **"Enable MFA"**
3. Scan the QR code with your authenticator app
4. Enter the 6-digit code to verify
5. Save your backup codes in a safe place

### Password Management

- **Change Password**: Settings > Security > Change Password
- **Forgot Password**: Click "Forgot Password" on login page
- **Password Requirements**: 8+ characters, mixed case, numbers, symbols

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### Login Problems

**Can't log in with email/password:**
- Check if your email is verified
- Try "Forgot Password" to reset
- Ensure caps lock is off
- Clear browser cache and cookies

**MFA not working:**
- Check your authenticator app time sync
- Try using backup codes
- Contact support if codes are lost

#### Learning Platform Issues

**Videos not loading:**
- Check your internet connection
- Try a different browser
- Clear browser cache
- Disable ad blockers temporarily

**Assessments not submitting:**
- Ensure stable internet connection
- Don't refresh the page during submission
- Check that all questions are answered

#### Payment Issues

**Transaction failed:**
- Verify sufficient token balance
- Check network congestion
- Try again in a few minutes
- Contact support for stuck transactions
