**Where Intelligence Meets Creativity**

### The Revolutionary Concept

**BuildSpaces** are specialized rooms at The Citadel where students enter a fully immersive learning and building environment powered by Elara AI. Each room is a self-contained universe of intelligence, creativity, and productivity.

**Think**: Tony Stark's workshop meets Hogwarts' Room of Requirement meets MIT Media Lab.

### The Experience

**When you enter a BuildSpace:**

1. **Elara Greets You**
   - Voice-activated AI assistant
   - Knows your learning history
   - Understands your current project
   - Adapts to your learning style

2. **The Room Transforms**
   - Displays adjust to your needs
