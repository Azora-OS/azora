## üéØ EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Azora is building the world's first **self-sustaining entrepreneurship ecosystem** powered by a Development Fund model that aligns incentives between students, the platform, and the community.

**Key Innovation**: Students don't give up equity. They contribute 10% of their success to a transparent Development Fund that improves the tools and education that made them successful.

**Result**: A perpetual flywheel where each success creates more success, funding better tools, education, and opportunities for the next generation.

## üí° THE MODEL

### Traditional VC/Accelerator Model ‚ùå

**Y Combinator / Traditional Accelerators:**
- Take 7-10% equity in your company
- Provide $125K-$500K funding
- 3-month program
- Limited to companies they can manually support
- **You lose ownership forever**

**Problems:**
- Feels extractive
- Limited scale (manual mentorship)
- Equity dilution
- No ongoing benefit after program

### Azora Development Fund Model ‚úÖ

**Azora Ecosystem:**
- Take 0% equity (you keep 100% ownership)
- Provide free/low-cost education + AzStudio tools
- Continuous support (AI-powered, scales infinitely)
- 10% of revenue/exit goes to Development Fund
- **You contribute to the ecosystem that made you successful**

**Benefits:**
- Feels collaborative (not extractive)
- Scales infinitely (software doesn't get tired)
- No equity dilution
- Ongoing ecosystem improvements benefit YOU

## üíé THE LEGAL STRUCTURE

### Not Equity - Development Fee

**Structure:**
- "10% of revenue or exit proceeds goes to Azora Development Fund"
- NOT "We own 10% of your company"
- You retain 100% ownership and control
- Clear, simple, enforceable

**Payment Triggers:**
- Revenue-based: 10% of monthly/annual revenue
- Exit-based: 10% of acquisition/IPO proceeds
- Flexible: Choose what works for your business model

**Protections:**
- Cap on total contribution (e.g., 10x initial value)
- Deferral options for struggling companies
- Forgiveness for failed companies
- **Fair and founder-friendly**

### Tax Advantages

**Potential Benefits:**
- May be deductible as platform/service fee
- Not equity dilution (no cap table impact)
- Clear expense categorization
- Consult with tax professional

### Comparison to Alternatives

| Model | Equity Taken | Ownership | Ongoing Benefit | Scale |
|-------|--------------|-----------|-----------------|-------|
| **Y Combinator** | 7% | You lose 7% | Limited | Manual |
| **Traditional VC** | 20-40% | You lose control | None | Limited |
| **Revenue Share** | 0% | You keep 100% | None | Limited |
| **Azora Fund** | 0% | You keep 100% | Continuous | Infinite |
