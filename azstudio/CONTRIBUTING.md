# Contributing to AzStudio

Welcome, and thank you for your interest in contributing to AzStudio!

We are building the next generation of AI-powered development tools, and your help is invaluable.

## Ways to Contribute

There are several ways you can get involved:

1.  **Reporting Issues**: Help us identify bugs and improve stability.
2.  **Feature Requests**: Share your ideas for making AzStudio better.
3.  **Documentation**: Improve our docs to help other developers.
4.  **Code Contributions**: Fix bugs or implement new features.

## Reporting Issues

If you find a bug or have a feature request, please report it on our [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/azora-technologies/azstudio/issues) page.

### Writing Good Bug Reports

-   **Use a clear title**: Describe the issue concisely.
-   **Provide reproduction steps**: Numbered steps to reproduce the bug.
-   **Expected vs. Actual**: What should have happened vs. what did happen.
-   **Environment details**: OS version, AzStudio version, etc.
-   **Screenshots/Video**: Visual aids are very helpful.

## Development Workflow

1.  **Fork the repository** on GitHub.
2.  **Clone your fork** locally.
3.  **Create a branch** for your changes.
4.  **Install dependencies**: Run `npm install`.
5.  **Build and Run**: Follow the instructions in `README.md` to build and run AzStudio from source.
6.  **Make your changes**.
7.  **Run tests**: Ensure your changes don't break existing functionality.
8.  **Submit a Pull Request**.

## Code of Conduct

We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for all. Please treat everyone with respect and professionalism.
