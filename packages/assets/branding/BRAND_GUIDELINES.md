# AZORA OS - Brand Guidelines

## Brand Identity

### Vision
Azora OS represents the future of planetary-scale intelligence - a living, breathing ecosystem that transforms how humanity interacts with technology, education, and each other.

### Mission
To create an autonomous, self-evolving operating system that empowers every individual on Earth with access to advanced AI, education, and financial opportunities.

### Brand Values
- **Intelligence**: Advanced AI that learns and evolves
- **Accessibility**: Technology for everyone, everywhere
- **Autonomy**: Self-governing and self-improving systems
- **Unity**: Connecting humanity through shared intelligence
- **Innovation**: Pushing the boundaries of what's possible

## Visual Identity

### Logo Usage
- Primary logo: `Azora OS Logo.png`
- Minimum size: 32px height for digital, 1 inch for print
- Clear space: Minimum 1/2 logo height on all sides
- Never stretch, rotate, or modify the logo proportions

### Color Palette

#### Primary Colors
- **Azora Blue**: #0066FF (RGB: 0, 102, 255)
- **Neural Purple**: #6B46C1 (RGB: 107, 70, 193)
- **Quantum Green**: #10B981 (RGB: 16, 185, 129)

#### Secondary Colors
- **Deep Space**: #1E1B4B (RGB: 30, 27, 75)
- **Stellar White**: #FFFFFF (RGB: 255, 255, 255)
- **Cosmic Gray**: #6B7280 (RGB: 107, 114, 128)

#### Accent Colors
- **Energy Orange**: #F59E0B (RGB: 245, 158, 11)
- **Plasma Pink**: #EC4899 (RGB: 236, 72, 153)
- **Cyber Cyan**: #06B6D4 (RGB: 6, 182, 212)

### Typography

#### Primary Font: Inter
- Headings: Inter Bold/Semi-Bold
- Body text: Inter Regular
- Code: Inter Mono

#### Fallback Fonts
- System fonts: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Segoe UI', Roboto, sans-serif
- Monospace: 'SF Mono', Monaco, 'Cascadia Code', monospace

### Iconography
- Style: Minimalist, geometric, futuristic
- Line weight: 2px
- Corner radius: 4px
- Colors: Use brand palette
- Format: SVG preferred, PNG for complex icons

## Voice & Tone

### Brand Voice
- **Intelligent**: Knowledgeable without being condescending
- **Empowering**: Inspiring confidence and capability
- **Accessible**: Clear, jargon-free communication
- **Visionary**: Forward-thinking and aspirational
- **Human**: Warm, relatable, and understanding

### Tone Guidelines
- **Professional**: Maintain credibility and expertise
- **Friendly**: Approachable and welcoming
- **Confident**: Assured but not arrogant
- **Inclusive**: Language that welcomes everyone
- **Innovative**: Exciting about the future

## Brand Protection

### Do's
✅ Use official brand assets
✅ Maintain consistent spacing and proportions
✅ Follow color guidelines precisely
✅ Use approved fonts and typography
✅ Maintain brand voice in all communications

### Don'ts
❌ Modify or recreate the logo
❌ Use unauthorized colors or fonts
❌ Stretch or distort brand elements
❌ Use low-resolution or pixelated assets
❌ Combine with competing brand elements
