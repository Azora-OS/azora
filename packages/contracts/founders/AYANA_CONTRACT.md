## 9. Design Principles

### Azora Design Manifesto:

**1. User-First**
- Design for real people and real needs
- Test with actual users
- Iterate based on feedback

**2. Accessible by Default**
- WCAG 2.1 AA minimum
- Design for low bandwidth
- Support diverse abilities

**3. African Context**
- Understand local needs and constraints
- Culturally appropriate design
- Language and literacy considerations

**4. Beautiful & Functional**
- Aesthetics matter
- But never sacrifice usability
- Form follows function

**5. Systematic**
- Build reusable systems
- Document everything
- Enable design at scale

**6. Data-Informed**
- Let metrics guide decisions
- But trust design intuition
- Balance art and science
