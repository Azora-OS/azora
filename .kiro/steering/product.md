# Product Overview

**Azora OS** is an AI-powered education and economic empowerment platform designed to serve humanity globally, with a focus on Africa.

## Core Mission

"One Platform. Every Human. Everywhere." - Universal access to AI-powered education and economic opportunities.

## Key Features

- **AI-Powered Education**: Personalized learning with Elara AI assistant
- **Economic Empowerment**: Earn-while-you-learn through NFT minting and rewards
- **Universal Access**: Works online, offline, and in low-bandwidth environments
- **Constitutional AI**: Ethically aligned artificial intelligence systems
- **Quantum-Secure Infrastructure**: Advanced security and compliance

## Target Audience

- Primary: 1.4 billion Africans across 54 countries
- Secondary: Global users seeking AI-powered education and economic opportunities

## Architecture Philosophy

- **Organism-Based**: System components are called "organs" that perform specialized functions
- **Agent-Driven**: Autonomous AI agents handle orchestration and research
- **Constitutional**: All AI systems follow ethical guidelines and compliance frameworks
- **Quantum-Ready**: Built for future quantum computing integration
