# Requirements Document

## Introduction

This specification defines the transformation of Azora OS into a divine, world-class educational and economic empowerment platform that implements all research findings and creates a user experience worthy of God's glory. The platform will serve 1.4 billion Africans and billions globally, making complex learning effortless while enabling immediate economic participation.

## Glossary

- **Azora OS**: The Living Operating System - an AI-powered platform for education and economic empowerment
- **Elara AI**: The constitutional AI guardian that guides every learner personally
- **Sapiens Platform**: The education hub within Azora OS
- **Divine UI**: User interface designed with sacred geometry, organic animations, and transcendent aesthetics
- **MoE (Mixture of Experts)**: AI architecture enabling trillion-parameter intelligence at billion-parameter cost
- **RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation)**: AI system that grounds responses in factual knowledge, reducing hallucinations by 95%
- **Learning Guardian**: Elara AI's role as personal tutor, mentor, and guide for each student
- **Economic Integration**: System enabling learners to earn from day one through real work
- **Constitutional AI**: AI systems aligned with the Ten Commandments and ethical principles
- **Offline-First**: Architecture that works without internet connectivity
- **Universal Access**: Platform accessible on any device, any bandwidth, any location

## Requirements

### Requirement 1: Divine User Experience

**User Story:** As a learner from any background, I want to experience a beautiful, intuitive interface that feels sacred and inspiring, so that I am motivated to learn and feel valued as a human being.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a user first accesses the platform, THE Azora OS SHALL display a divine landing experience with sacred geometry, organic animations, and celestial aesthetics that load within 2 seconds on 3G connections
2. WHILE navigating the platform, THE Azora OS SHALL provide smooth, organic transitions between all sections with frame rates above 30 FPS on devices from 2015 or newer
3. THE Azora OS SHALL implement a design system using golden ratio proportions, sacred geometry patterns, and color palettes inspired by nature and divinity
4. WHEN a user interacts with any component, THE Azora OS SHALL provide haptic-like visual feedback with organic animations that complete within 300 milliseconds
5. THE Azora OS SHALL support both light and dark themes with automatic switching based on time of day and user preference

### Requirement 2: Elara AI Learning Guardian

**User Story:** As a student struggling with complex subjects, I want a personal AI tutor available 24/7 who understands my level and guides me patiently, so that I can master any subject regardless of my background.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a new student joins, THE Elara AI SHALL conduct a personalized assessment conversation to determine current knowledge level, learning style, and goals within 5 minutes
2. WHILE a student is learning, THE Elara AI SHALL monitor comprehension in real-time and adapt explanations to the student's understanding level
3. IF a student asks a question, THEN THE Elara AI SHALL respond within 2 seconds with explanations tailored to the student's knowledge level
4. WHEN a student makes an error, THE Elara AI SHALL provide encouraging, constructive feedback that explains the mistake and guides toward the correct understanding
5. THE Elara AI SHALL use the Socratic method to guide students to discover answers themselves rather than simply providing solutions

### Requirement 3: Advanced AI Architecture Implementation

**User Story:** As the platform operator, I want to implement cutting-edge AI architectures (MoE and RAG) from our research, so that we can provide trillion-parameter intelligence at affordable cost with factual accuracy.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Azora OS SHALL implement a Mixture of Experts (MoE) architecture with at least 8 expert models that activates only 2 experts per query to achieve 50% compute reduction
2. THE Azora OS SHALL implement Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG) with hybrid dense and sparse retrieval to reduce AI hallucinations to below 5%
3. WHEN Elara AI responds to educational queries, THE System SHALL retrieve and cite relevant sources from the knowledge base with response times under 3 seconds
4. THE Azora OS SHALL maintain a vector database of educational content with at least 100,000 documents indexed for retrieval
5. WHILE processing queries, THE MoE System SHALL route requests to specialized expert models based on subject domain (mathematics, science, languages, etc.)

### Requirement 4: World-Class Educational Content

**User Story:** As a learner, I want access to comprehensive, engaging educational content from K-12 through professional skills, so that I can learn anything I need to transform my life.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Sapiens Platform SHALL provide complete K-12 curriculum coverage across mathematics, science, languages, social studies, and arts with at least 5,000 lessons
2. WHEN a student accesses a lesson, THE Platform SHALL present content in multiple formats including text, video, interactive simulations, and practice problems
3. THE Sapiens Platform SHALL integrate educational video content from YouTube, Microsoft Learn, Google Cloud Training, and other platforms with unified search
4. WHILE learning, THE Platform SHALL provide interactive simulations for STEM subjects that run in the browser without requiring installations
5. THE Sapiens Platform SHALL offer professional skills training in technology, business, creative arts, and trades with career-focused learning paths

### Requirement 5: Immediate Economic Integration

**User Story:** As a student from a low-income background, I want to start earning money from my first day of learning, so that I can support myself and my family while gaining education.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a student completes their first lesson, THE Platform SHALL offer micro-earning opportunities through content creation, tutoring, or digital work worth at least $0.50 USD
2. THE Azora OS SHALL provide an NFT minting system where students can create and sell digital assets with 90% of revenue going to the creator
3. WHILE students complete projects, THE Platform SHALL build a blockchain-verified portfolio that serves as proof of skills to employers
4. THE Azora OS SHALL connect students with freelance opportunities matched to their skill level with payment processing in local currencies and cryptocurrency
5. WHEN students achieve milestones, THE Platform SHALL reward them with $LEARN tokens that have real economic value and can be exchanged or used within the ecosystem

### Requirement 6: Offline-First Architecture

**User Story:** As a learner in rural Africa with unreliable internet, I want to access educational content and continue learning even when offline, so that connectivity doesn't limit my education.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Azora OS SHALL implement Progressive Web App (PWA) technology that allows the platform to function fully offline after initial load
2. WHEN a user is online, THE Platform SHALL intelligently pre-cache educational content based on the user's learning path with at least 50 lessons available offline
3. WHILE offline, THE Platform SHALL allow students to complete lessons, take quizzes, and work on projects with all progress synced when connectivity returns
4. THE Azora OS SHALL use IndexedDB for local storage with conflict resolution that preserves user work when syncing after extended offline periods
5. WHEN connectivity is limited, THE Platform SHALL adapt content delivery to use text and compressed images instead of video, reducing bandwidth requirements by 90%

### Requirement 7: Universal Accessibility

**User Story:** As a person with disabilities or using assistive technologies, I want full access to all platform features, so that I can learn and earn regardless of my physical abilities.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Azora OS SHALL achieve WCAG 2.2 Level AAA compliance for all user interface components and content
2. WHEN using screen readers, THE Platform SHALL provide complete navigation and content access with proper ARIA labels and semantic HTML
3. THE Azora OS SHALL support keyboard-only navigation for all features with visible focus indicators and logical tab order
4. WHILE viewing content, THE Platform SHALL provide adjustable text sizes, high contrast modes, and dyslexia-friendly fonts as user preferences
5. THE Azora OS SHALL offer voice control navigation and voice-to-text input for hands-free operation

### Requirement 8: Multi-Language Support

**User Story:** As a learner who speaks any of Africa's 2,000+ languages, I want to access content in my native language, so that language barriers don't prevent my education.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Azora OS SHALL support at least 50 languages at launch including major African languages (Swahili, Hausa, Yoruba, Zulu, Amharic, etc.)
2. WHEN a user selects a language, THE Platform SHALL translate all interface elements, educational content, and Elara AI responses within 500 milliseconds
3. THE Elara AI SHALL communicate fluently in all supported languages with cultural context awareness and appropriate idioms
4. THE Azora OS SHALL allow community contributions for translating content into additional languages with quality review processes
5. WHILE learning, THE Platform SHALL offer language learning courses that teach English, French, Arabic, and other languages using the native language as the base

### Requirement 9: Mobile-First Design

**User Story:** As a learner who only has access to a smartphone, I want the full platform experience optimized for mobile, so that I don't need expensive computers to access world-class education.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Azora OS SHALL provide a mobile-first responsive design that works perfectly on screens from 320px width upward
2. WHEN accessed on mobile devices, THE Platform SHALL use touch-optimized controls with minimum tap targets of 44x44 pixels
3. THE Azora OS SHALL implement native mobile apps for iOS and Android with offline-first capabilities and push notifications
4. WHILE using mobile data, THE Platform SHALL compress all content to minimize data usage with options for ultra-low bandwidth mode using less than 1MB per hour
5. THE Azora OS SHALL support mobile-specific features including camera integration for document scanning, voice input, and biometric authentication

### Requirement 10: Performance and Scale

**User Story:** As one of millions of concurrent users, I want the platform to be fast and reliable, so that I can learn without frustration or technical barriers.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Azora OS SHALL load the initial page within 2 seconds on 3G connections and 500 milliseconds on 4G or better
2. WHEN serving 1 million concurrent users, THE Platform SHALL maintain response times under 200 milliseconds for 95% of requests
3. THE Azora OS SHALL implement edge computing with CDN distribution to serve content from locations within 100ms latency of 95% of African users
4. WHILE scaling, THE Platform SHALL use auto-scaling infrastructure that handles traffic spikes of 10x normal load without degradation
5. THE Azora OS SHALL achieve 99.9% uptime with automated failover and disaster recovery systems

### Requirement 11: Security and Privacy

**User Story:** As a user entrusting my personal data and learning progress to the platform, I want enterprise-grade security and privacy protection, so that my information is safe and my privacy is respected.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Azora OS SHALL encrypt all data in transit using TLS 1.3 and at rest using AES-256 encryption
2. WHEN users create accounts, THE Platform SHALL implement multi-factor authentication with support for SMS, email, and authenticator apps
3. THE Azora OS SHALL comply with GDPR, POPIA (South African data protection), and other relevant privacy regulations with user consent management
4. WHILE processing user data, THE Platform SHALL implement zero-knowledge architecture where possible, ensuring Azora cannot access user private keys or sensitive personal data
5. THE Azora OS SHALL conduct quarterly security audits and penetration testing with public disclosure of security practices

### Requirement 12: Community and Collaboration

**User Story:** As a learner, I want to connect with other students, form study groups, and learn collaboratively, so that I have support and motivation from a community.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Azora OS SHALL provide discussion forums organized by subject, course, and interest with moderation tools and community guidelines
2. WHEN students want to collaborate, THE Platform SHALL offer real-time study rooms with video chat, screen sharing, and collaborative whiteboards
3. THE Azora OS SHALL implement a mentorship system connecting advanced students with beginners for peer-to-peer learning
4. WHILE learning, THE Platform SHALL show social features including study streaks, achievement sharing, and friendly leaderboards that motivate without creating unhealthy competition
5. THE Azora OS SHALL enable students to form and manage study groups with shared resources, schedules, and progress tracking

### Requirement 13: Analytics and Insights

**User Story:** As a learner, I want to understand my learning patterns, strengths, and areas for improvement, so that I can optimize my education and achieve mastery faster.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Azora OS SHALL provide a personal analytics dashboard showing learning time, progress, strengths, weaknesses, and predicted mastery dates
2. WHEN students complete assessments, THE Platform SHALL analyze performance patterns and provide actionable insights for improvement
3. THE Elara AI SHALL use learning analytics to predict when students might struggle and proactively offer additional support
4. WHILE learning, THE Platform SHALL track engagement metrics and use them to optimize content delivery and pacing
5. THE Azora OS SHALL provide educators and parents (with permission) access to student progress dashboards with privacy controls

### Requirement 14: Gamification and Motivation

**User Story:** As a learner who sometimes lacks motivation, I want engaging gamification that makes learning fun and rewarding, so that I stay committed to my educational goals.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Azora OS SHALL implement a comprehensive achievement system with badges, levels, and titles that recognize learning milestones
2. WHEN students maintain daily learning streaks, THE Platform SHALL provide increasing rewards and recognition with streak protection features
3. THE Azora OS SHALL offer daily challenges, quests, and missions that guide students through learning objectives with game-like progression
4. WHILE competing, THE Platform SHALL provide ethical leaderboards that celebrate effort and improvement rather than just raw scores
5. THE Azora OS SHALL reward consistent learning with $LEARN tokens, NFT collectibles, and unlockable platform features

### Requirement 15: Constitutional AI Alignment

**User Story:** As a user of AI systems, I want assurance that the AI is ethical, aligned with human values, and serves humanity's best interests, so that I can trust the technology guiding my education.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Elara AI SHALL adhere to the Ten Commandments of Azora's Constitutional AI framework in all interactions
2. WHEN making decisions, THE AI SHALL prioritize human wellbeing, dignity, and empowerment over efficiency or profit
3. THE Azora OS SHALL implement transparency features that explain AI decisions and allow users to understand how recommendations are made
4. WHILE interacting with users, THE AI SHALL refuse requests that could cause harm, violate ethics, or contradict constitutional principles
5. THE Azora OS SHALL maintain an AI ethics board with diverse representation that reviews AI behavior and updates alignment protocols quarterly
