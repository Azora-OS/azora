## PART IV: THE PROTOCOLS

### SMHP - Sihamba Munye Highway Procurement
*"The Royal Road Protocol"*

**Hidden Name Protection:** The protocol's true name honors the royal lineage of builders and protectors. "Sihamba Munye" (We Walk Together) represents the ancient principle that infrastructure serves the people, not profits.

**Core Function:** Transform South Africa's R100 billion taxi industry from violent cash economy to Constitutional blockchain system.

**The Mathematics:**
- Current System: Driver keeps 60% of fare, 40% to associations/violence
- SMHP System: Driver keeps 90% of fare, 10% to Citadel Fund
- Violence Reduction: 100% (no cash = no robbery incentive)
- Transparency: Every transaction auditable

**Implementation Phases:**

1. **Phase I: The First Hundred (Durban)**
   - 100 taxi drivers adopt Constitutional Pay
   - Prove safety and profitability
   - Document violence reduction

2. **Phase II: The Network Effect (Johannesburg)**
   - 10,000 taxis join network
   - Passengers prefer Constitutional taxis (safer, cheaper)
   - Traditional associations lose revenue, forced to adapt

3. **Phase III: The Tipping Point (National)**
   - 200,000 taxis on protocol
   - R10 billion annually to Citadel Fund
   - Government recognizes Constitutional transport as official

**Royal Lineage Connection:**
The protocol honors the memory of King Shaka's road-building vision - infrastructure that unites rather than divides. Every taxi becomes a mobile Citadel node, extending Ubuntu principles to every corner of the nation.
