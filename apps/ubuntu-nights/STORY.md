## ğŸ® Core Gameplay Loop (MVP - 1 Hour)

### Mission 1: "The Message"
- **Location**: Your apartment in the township
- **Objective**: Receive Mama Zola's message, learn basic controls
- **AI Agent**: ELARA guides you through the interface
- **Duration**: 5 minutes

### Mission 2: "Street Wisdom"
- **Location**: Market district
- **Objective**: Help 3 vendors with small tasks (fetch, deliver, protect)
- **Mechanic**: Each task increases Ubuntu Score
- **AI Agent**: KOFI helps you optimize routes
- **Duration**: 10 minutes

### Mission 3: "The Hack"
- **Location**: Abandoned Nexus data center
- **Objective**: Infiltrate and extract evidence of corruption
- **Mechanic**: Stealth-based, non-lethal
- **AI Agent**: SANKOFA provides historical context on Nexus crimes
- **Duration**: 15 minutes

### Mission 4: "Community Court"
- **Location**: The Citadel (community center)
- **Objective**: Present evidence, community votes on action
- **Mechanic**: Dialogue choices affect outcome
- **AI Agent**: UBUNTU moderates the discussion
- **Duration**: 10 minutes

### Final Mission: "The Stand"
- **Location**: City streets
- **Objective**: Organize peaceful protest OR defensive action
- **Mechanic**: Your Ubuntu Score determines community turnout
- **AI Agents**: ALL 5 agents coordinate the resistance
- **Duration**: 20 minutes

**Total MVP Playtime: ~60 minutes**
