## ðŸ’Ž The Proof of Value System

### Core Mechanic: Value as Life Force

**Philosophy:**
> "In Azania, your life force isn't blood - it's your Proof of Value. Your contribution to the collective. Your Ubuntu Score made tangible."

**The Meter:**
- **Everyone has a Proof of Value (PoV) meter** (0-100%)
- **PoV represents:** Your contribution to society, your Ubuntu alignment, your right to exist in the system
- **High PoV:** You're contributing, building, teaching, helping
- **Low PoV:** You're extracting, corrupting, harming
- **Zero PoV:** You're sent to your maker for reincarnation and reset

**Visual Design:**
- Glowing aura around characters (gold = high PoV, red = low PoV)
- Percentage displayed above head
- Pulsing energy field (stronger = higher value)
- Constitutional AI constantly calculates in real-time

**How You Gain PoV:**
- Help community members (+5% per action)
- Build AOLnet infrastructure (+10%)
- Teach others (+8%)
- Resolve conflicts peacefully (+15%)
- Complete missions (+20%)
- Convert enemies to allies (+25%)

**How You Lose PoV:**
- Harm innocents (-20%)
- Lie to community (-15%)
- Destroy property (-10%)
- Act selfishly (-5%)
- Get hit by enemy beams (-10% per hit)

## âš¡ The Beam System

### What It Looks Like

**Visual:**
- **Beam of Light** (not bullets, not blood)
- **Golden/Blue energy** streams
- **Harmonic sound** (not gunshots)
- **Peaceful dissolution** (enemy fades into light particles)

**How Beams Work:**
```
1. Player aims beam weapon
2. Beam connects to target
3. Target's PoV meter drains (-10% per second)
4. When PoV reaches 0%:
   - Target glows with inner light
   - Target dissolves into particles
   - Particles rise upward (ascending)
   - Flash: "Reincarnation Initiated - PoV Reset"
5. Target respawns with 50% PoV at nearest spawn point
```

**Strategic Depth:**
- **High PoV enemies** (80-100%) take longer to beam (they've earned their existence)
- **Low PoV enemies** (0-30%) beam quickly (they're already failing the system)
- **Corrupt officials** start with negative PoV (instant beam, but high Ubuntu Score penalty)
- **Innocent civilians** have 100% PoV (beaming them = massive penalty)

**No Gore. No Blood. No Suffering.**

## ðŸ”„ The Reincarnation Mechanic

### For Enemies (NPCs)

**When an enemy's PoV reaches 0%:**

1. **Reincarnation Triggered** - No pain, no suffering
2. **Reincarnation Screen** (brief flash):
   ```
   "Agent [Name]'s Proof of Value depleted.
   Sent to their maker for reset.
   They will return with 50% PoV.
   Ubuntu Score: +5 (Conflict Resolution)"
   ```
3. **Enemy Respawns** - Same enemy returns later, but:
   - **PoV Reset to 50%** (fresh start)
   - **Remembers you** (dialogue changes)
   - **May become ally** (if you showed mercy before)
   - **Learns from experience** (smarter tactics)

**Mercy Mechanic:**
- If enemy's PoV is below 30%, you can **spare them** instead of beaming
- Sparing restores their PoV to 50% and converts them to ally
- **Massive Ubuntu Score bonus** (+50)

**Example:**
```
First Encounter:
  Corrupt Guard: "Stop! You can't enter here!"
  [Player beams guard]
  Guard: *dissolves into light* "I... see now..."

Second Encounter (30 minutes later):
  Same Guard: "You... you showed me the truth. I'm with the Citadel now."
  [Guard becomes ally]
```

### For Players (Death System)

**When YOUR PoV reaches 0%:**

1. **Reincarnation Experience** (30 seconds):
   - Screen fades to white
   - You meet **The Maker** (abstract, peaceful entity)
   - The Maker shows you:
     - **Your PoV history** (what you built vs. what you destroyed)
     - **Ubuntu Score impact** of your actions
     - **Lessons learned** this life
   - You choose **how to reincarnate**

2. **Reincarnation Choices:**
   - **Same Location** (quick respawn, PoV reset to 30%, -10 Ubuntu Score)
   - **Nearest Citadel** (safe respawn, PoV reset to 50%, no penalty)
   - **With Wisdom** (respawn with hint, PoV reset to 70%, +5 Ubuntu Score)

3. **Consequences:**
   - **High Ubuntu Score (800+):** Reincarnate with 80% PoV + buffs
   - **Medium Ubuntu Score (400-799):** Reincarnate with 50% PoV
   - **Low Ubuntu Score (0-399):** Reincarnate with 30% PoV + debuffs

**The Message:** Your value to society determines how strong you return.

**The Message:** Death teaches. Every reincarnation is a chance to do better.

## ðŸŽ® Combat Mechanics (Action-Packed but Meaningful)

### Weapon Types (All Non-Lethal in Spirit)

**1. Truth Beam (Default)**
- **Function:** Drains enemy PoV (-10%/second)
- **Ammo:** Unlimited (powered by your own PoV)
- **Cost:** Drains 1% of YOUR PoV per second of use
- **Special:** Higher Ubuntu Score = more efficient (less PoV drain)
- **Strategy:** Don't spam - you're spending your own value

**2. Harmony Pulse**
- **Function:** Area-of-effect PoV freeze
- **Effect:** Enemies' PoV stops draining for 5 seconds (gives them time to reconsider)
- **Cost:** 10% of YOUR PoV
- **Visual:** Rippling golden wave
- **Use Case:** Non-violent crowd control, gives enemies chance to surrender

**3. Wisdom Bolt**
- **Function:** Instantly restores enemy PoV to 100% and converts to ally
- **Effect:** Enemy sees the truth, joins your side
- **Cost:** 30% of YOUR PoV (you're giving them your value)
- **Requirement:** Ubuntu Score 600+
- **Visual:** Bright blue lightning that enlightens
- **Philosophy:** You sacrifice your value to elevate others

**4. Sankofa Shield**
- **Function:** Defensive barrier
- **Effect:** Reflects attacks back as healing light
- **Visual:** Holographic African patterns
- **Philosophy:** Defense, not aggression

**5. Ubuntu Link**
- **Function:** Connect with allies to share PoV
- **Effect:** All linked players' PoV averages out and regenerates faster
- **Visual:** Golden threads connecting players
- **Philosophy:** Stronger together - collective value exceeds individual
- **Mechanic:** 4 players at 50% PoV each = all become 75% when linked

### Scenario 1: "The Citadel Defense"

**Setup:**
- 50 corrupt agents attack BuildSpaces HQ
- You and 3 allies defend
- Waves of enemies

**Gameplay:**
- **Fast-paced shooting** (beams, not bullets)
- **Parkour movement** (dodge, slide, climb)
- **Strategic positioning** (high ground advantage)
- **Team coordination** (Ubuntu Link for power)

**PoV Dynamics:**
- Enemies start with 60% PoV (they're misguided, not evil)
- Every enemy you beam to 0% respawns angrier (harder to convert)
- Every enemy you spare (below 30% PoV) becomes ally
- **Strategic choice:** Beam fast (easy) or spare (harder but better)

**Twist:**
- If you spare 30+ enemies, they form a **Community Shield**
- Final wave: Your converted enemies **fight with you**
- Their combined PoV **boosts yours** (Ubuntu Link effect)

**Result:**
- **High Ubuntu Score:** You win with an army of allies, everyone at 100% PoV
- **Low Ubuntu Score:** You win alone at 20% PoV, exhausted
