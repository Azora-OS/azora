## Self-Declaration Option

For accessibility, we offer **instant approval** via self-declaration:

**Example:**
```
I am currently a full-time university student with no regular income. 
I rely on family support for my education and living expenses.
```

**Requirements:**
- Minimum 50 characters
- Honest statement about your situation
- Auto-approved instantly

---

## Document Upload Process

1. **Take Photo/Scan**: Clear, readable image
2. **Upload**: Supported formats: JPG, PNG, PDF
3. **Review**: 24-48 hour review by our team
4. **Notification**: Email when approved/rejected

---

## Verification Validity

- **Valid for**: 1 year from approval
- **Renewal**: Re-verify annually
- **Upgrade**: Can upgrade to Standard anytime (no verification)
- **Downgrade**: Submit new verification if circumstances change

## Fraud Prevention

While we trust our students, we monitor for:
- Multiple accounts with same documents
- Suspicious patterns
- Mismatched information

**Consequences of fraud:**
- Account suspension
- Upgrade to Standard pricing
- Possible legal action for severe cases

## FAQs

**Q: What if I don't have documents?**
A: Use the self-declaration option - it's auto-approved.

**Q: How long does document review take?**
A: Typically 24-48 hours, sometimes faster.

**Q: Can I start learning while verification is pending?**
A: Yes! You'll be charged Standard pricing until approved, then refunded the difference.

**Q: What if my verification is rejected?**
A: You can resubmit with different documents or use self-declaration.

**Q: Is self-declaration really auto-approved?**
A: Yes, instantly. We trust our students and believe in accessibility.

**Q: What if my income changes?**
A: You can upgrade anytime. For downgrades, submit new verification.
