## OUR PLEDGE

In the spirit of Ubuntu ("I am because we are"), we pledge to make participation in Azora OS a harassment-free, inclusive, and empowering experience for everyone, regardless of:

- Age, body size, disability, ethnicity
- Gender identity and expression
- Level of experience, education, socio-economic status
- Nationality, personal appearance, race, religion
- Sexual identity and orientation

We are committed to creating a community where technology serves humanity and individual success multiplies collective prosperity.

---

## OUR STANDARDS

### Positive Behaviors ‚úÖ

**We encourage**:
- **Ubuntu Spirit**: "I am because we are" - supporting each other's growth
- **Truth as Currency**: Operating with honesty and transparency
- **Collective Benefit**: Considering community impact in all actions
- **Respectful Communication**: Using welcoming and inclusive language

### Unacceptable Behaviors ‚ùå

**We prohibit**:
- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination of any kind
- Trolling, insulting comments, or personal attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Sexual language, imagery, or unwelcome advances
- Violent threats or language
- Inciting others to engage in prohibited behavior
- Constitutional violations or unethical conduct
- Spreading misinformation or fake data (No Mock Protocol)
- Any conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## ENFORCEMENT

### Reporting Violations

**How to Report**:
- **Email**: conduct@azora.world
- **Discord**: Contact moderators
- **GitHub**: Use report feature

**What to Include**:
- Your contact information
- Description of incident
- Date, time, and location
- Names of people involved
- Witnesses (if any)
- Any supporting evidence

**Confidentiality**: All reports are confidential.

### Response Process

1. **Acknowledgment** (24 hours)
   - Confirm receipt of report
   - Assign investigator

2. **Investigation** (7 days)
   - Gather information
   - Interview parties involved
   - Review evidence

3. **Decision** (3 days)
   - Determine if violation occurred
   - Decide appropriate action
   - Notify parties

4. **Action** (Immediate)
   - Implement consequences
   - Monitor compliance
   - Follow up as needed

### Consequences

Violations may result in:

**Level 1: Warning**
- Private written warning
- Explanation of violation
- Guidance for improvement

**Level 2: Temporary Ban**
- Temporary suspension (7-30 days)
- No interaction with community
- Review before reinstatement

**Level 3: Permanent Ban**
- Permanent removal from community
- No future participation
- Public announcement (if appropriate)

**Severity Factors**:
- Intent and impact
- Previous violations
- Response to feedback
- Constitutional alignment
- Community safety

## APPEALS

If you disagree with an enforcement decision:

1. **Submit Appeal** (within 14 days)
   - Email: appeals@azora.world
   - Explain your perspective
   - Provide new evidence

2. **Review** (14 days)
   - Independent review
   - Consider all information
   - Make final decision

3. **Decision** (Final)
   - Uphold, modify, or reverse
   - Notify all parties
   - Update records
