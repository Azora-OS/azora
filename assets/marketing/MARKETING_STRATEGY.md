# AZORA OS - Marketing Strategy & Campaign Assets

## Marketing Positioning

### Target Audiences

#### Primary Audience: Tech Innovators & Early Adopters
- Age: 25-45
- Profile: Software developers, tech entrepreneurs, AI researchers
- Pain Points: Limited access to advanced AI tools, fragmented development environments
- Value Proposition: "The world's first truly intelligent operating system"

#### Secondary Audience: Educational Institutions
- Profile: Universities, coding bootcamps, online learning platforms
- Pain Points: Outdated educational technology, lack of AI integration
- Value Proposition: "Democratizing AI education for the next generation"

#### Tertiary Audience: Enterprise Decision Makers
- Profile: CTOs, IT Directors, Innovation Leaders
- Pain Points: Legacy systems, AI integration challenges, scalability issues
- Value Proposition: "Enterprise-grade AI infrastructure that evolves with your business"
