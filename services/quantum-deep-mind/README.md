# Quantum Deep Mind Service

The Quantum Deep Mind Service is an advanced AI service for Azora OS that simulates consciousness and implements quantum neural networks.

## Features

- Quantum consciousness simulation
- Quantum neural network training
- Quantum cognition modeling
- Advanced pattern recognition and reasoning

## API Endpoints

- `GET /health` - Service health check
- `POST /api/simulate-consciousness` - Simulate quantum consciousness
- `POST /api/train-qnn` - Train quantum neural networks
- `POST /api/model-cognition` - Model quantum cognition processes

## Environment Variables

- `PORT` - Service port (default: 3013)
