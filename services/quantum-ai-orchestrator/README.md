# Quantum AI Orchestration Service

The Quantum AI Orchestration Service is a critical component of Azora OS that manages quantum computing resources and coordinates quantum AI workloads.

## Features

- Quantum circuit execution management
- Quantum machine learning training orchestration
- Hybrid classical-quantum workload distribution
- Quantum resource allocation and optimization

## API Endpoints

- `GET /health` - Service health check
- `POST /api/orchestrate` - Orchestrate quantum AI tasks
- `POST /api/execute-circuit` - Execute quantum circuits
- `POST /api/qml-train` - Train quantum machine learning models

## Environment Variables

- `PORT` - Service port (default: 3012)
